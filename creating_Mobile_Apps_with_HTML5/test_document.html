How to Make Green Eggs and Ham

Part 1: Get Set Up

You need the ingredients:

  1. One (1) ham.
  2. Two (2) eggs, well done.
  3. Spam: none.
  4. Green, for fun.

Part 2: Cook it

Here are the steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 230 F.
  2. Preset greenifier to maxmium green-ness.
  3. Put the ingredients onto a plate.
  4. Turn the oven off.
  5. Use a microwave instead.
  6. Place plate in microwave and set to 3 minutes on high heat.
  7. When done, insert plate of ingredients into greenifier for 10 seconds.
  8. Remove and add salt as needed before serving.

Part 3: Serve it

Now the presentation!

  1. Garnish plate as desired (leafy greens are preferable).
  2. Add utensils (green cutlery works best).
  3. Do a dance.
  4. Place plate on table.
  5. Smile!
